Industries We Serve

Expert project planning and scheduling across diverse sectors in the UK and EMEA. We understand your industry's unique challenges.

Construction

Navigate complex building projects with precision scheduling. We help contractors manage timelines, resources, and compliance across residential, commercial, and mixed-use developments.

Infrastructure

Support large-scale infrastructure programmes with expert planning. From highways to water systems, we deliver scheduling solutions that keep critical projects on track.

Energy

Manage renewable and conventional energy projects effectively. We specialise in scheduling power generation facilities, grid upgrades, and offshore developments with precision.

Rail

Coordinate complex rail projects including new lines, upgrades, and maintenance programmes. Our experience ensures schedule compliance and minimises disruption to operations.

IT & Technology

Deliver software and technology projects on schedule. We help teams manage complex dependencies, integrate systems, and maintain quality whilst meeting aggressive timelines.

Manufacturing

Optimise production facility projects and equipment installations. We schedule capital projects, upgrades, and operational improvements with minimal downtime impact.

Public Sector

Support government and public body initiatives with governance-compliant project planning. We understand public procurement, funding requirements, and reporting standards.

Architecture & Design Management

Help architectural practices and design managers deliver complex projects on time. We provide the project planning expertise that design-led firms need — from programme scheduling and progress tracking to subconsultant coordination and contractual compliance on large-scale commissions.

Middle East Mega-Projects

Support UK and international firms delivering landmark projects across Saudi Arabia, the UAE, and the wider EMEA region. From cultural masterplans and placemaking initiatives to giga-projects under Saudi Vision 2030, we bring structured scheduling discipline to ambitious programmes where design meets delivery at scale.
