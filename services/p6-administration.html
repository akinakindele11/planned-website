Primavera P6 & Oracle Primavera Cloud Administration Services

Expert setup, maintenance, and ongoing support for your P6 and Primavera Cloud systems

Get Started Today

Primavera P6 and Oracle Primavera Cloud are powerful tools, but they're also complex. Successful implementations require more than just purchasing the software. A well-administered P6 environment features secure access, reliable performance, appropriate configuration, effective backup and recovery, and responsive user support.

Many organisations struggle with P6 and Primavera Cloud administration, lacking in-house expertise or supporting multiple versions and deployments. This can lead to security vulnerabilities, performance degradation, user frustration, and lost productivity.

Our P6 administration services provide specialist expertise, proactive system management, and responsive support ensuring your P6 system operates reliably and securely. Whether you need full-service managed administration or targeted support for specific needs, we provide flexible engagement models tailored to your requirements.

What exactly is Primavera P6 administration and why does it matter?

P6 administration encompasses the technical and operational activities required to maintain a functioning, secure, and performant P6 environment. This includes installation and initial configuration, ongoing database maintenance, user and access management, system monitoring, backup and disaster recovery, patch and version management, performance tuning, and user support.

Administration matters because P6 systems are critical infrastructure for project-driven organisations. If P6 is unavailable, project managers cannot update schedules or access project data. If access controls are weak, sensitive project information is exposed to unauthorised access. If the system is poorly configured, users waste time working around limitations rather than accomplishing their jobs effectively.

Poor administration manifests in recognisable problems: slow system performance requiring workarounds, frequent crashes or outages, data integrity issues requiring corrections, security breaches or information leaks, user frustration and resistance to system adoption, difficulty supporting system upgrades or changes.

Good administration is often invisible—users access P6 reliably, their data is secure and backed up, the system is responsive, and they receive effective support. This invisibility doesn't mean administration is unimportant; it means it's working well.

Why should you outsource P6 administration instead of managing it internally?

Some organisations have the expertise and resources to manage P6 in-house. Many do not. Outsourcing P6 administration delivers several advantages.

Specialised Expertise: P6 administration requires deep knowledge of the system architecture, database management, security frameworks, and Oracle infrastructure. Maintaining this expertise internally requires dedicated specialists; outsourcing provides expert access without permanent headcount commitment.

Cost Efficiency: Full-time P6 administrators are expensive, particularly if you don't have sufficient volume to fully utilise their time. Outsourcing provides access to expertise on a flexible, usage-based model.

24/7 Availability: Critical system support often needs to happen outside normal business hours. Most organisations can't justify dedicated night/weekend coverage; outsourced providers can.

Proactive Management: Dedicated administrators proactively monitor systems, perform preventive maintenance, and identify issues before they impact users. In-house administrators often focus only on reactive problem-solving.

Best Practices: Outsourced administrators administer multiple P6 environments, experiencing diverse configurations, challenges, and solutions. This broad experience leads to best practice implementations.

Reduced Risk: System failures, data loss, or security breaches from inadequate administration can be catastrophic. Experienced administrators implement controls and redundancy minimising these risks.

Flexibility: As your needs change—scaling to new users, implementing new features, upgrading versions—outsourced providers scale with you without requiring internal restructuring.

What P6 administration services do you provide?

Our P6 administration offering covers the complete lifecycle of P6 systems:

Installation and Initial Setup

We install P6 in your environment (on-premise or cloud), configure databases and infrastructure, establish security frameworks, and optimise initial performance. Setup includes comprehensive documentation and handover to your operations team.

User and Access Management

We establish role-based access controls reflecting your organisation's structure and confidentiality requirements. We manage user provisioning and deprovisioning, enforce least-privilege access principles, and audit access to ensure appropriateness.

We also establish multi-factor authentication, manage API access and integrations, and implement controls preventing unauthorised data access or modification.

Security and Compliance

We implement security frameworks protecting P6 systems and data. This includes encryption for data at rest and in transit, network security and firewall configuration, vulnerability scanning and patch management, audit logging and monitoring, and compliance with relevant standards (ISO 27001, HIPAA, SOC 2, etc.).

We perform regular security reviews and penetration testing, ensuring systems remain secure against evolving threats.

Database Maintenance

We perform routine database maintenance including index optimisation, statistics updates, backup verification, and integrity checking. Regular maintenance prevents performance degradation and data corruption.

We also archive old project data appropriately, maintaining system performance and storage efficiency whilst preserving historical records.

Backup and Disaster Recovery

We establish comprehensive backup strategies including daily backups, off-site backup storage, and regular recovery testing. If disaster occurs—hardware failure, corruption, accidental deletion—we recover data with minimal loss.

We document and test disaster recovery procedures, ensuring you can recover from various failure scenarios. For critical systems, we implement redundancy and failover capabilities.

System Upgrades and Patch Management

We manage P6 upgrades and patches, testing changes in non-production environments, planning implementations carefully to minimise downtime, and providing post-implementation support.

We also manage OS, database, and middleware patches, ensuring systems remain current with security updates and supported versions.

Performance Monitoring and Tuning

We proactively monitor system performance, identifying bottlenecks and optimizing database queries, indexing, and infrastructure. Performance tuning ensures users have responsive systems enabling efficient work.

User Support and Help Desk

We provide technical support for P6 users including troubleshooting issues, explaining system functionality, recovering lost data, and resolving access problems. Support is available during project hours or 24/7 depending on engagement model.

Project Controls System Administration

Beyond core P6 administration, we support sophisticated project controls implementations including earned value management setup, custom reporting and dashboards, portfolio management configuration, and integrations with cost systems and other tools.

What's your approach to taking over P6 administration?

If you have existing P6 systems, transition to our management involves careful handoff and knowledge transfer.

We begin with a comprehensive assessment of current systems, reviewing infrastructure, configuration, security posture, backup procedures, and documentation. We identify any existing problems or risks requiring remediation.

We develop a transition plan specifying what will change, when changes will occur, and what coordination is required from your team. We minimise disruption to ongoing operations, coordinating implementation with your project schedules.

During transition, we document all systems, procedures, and configurations. We capture the knowledge of existing administrators, ensuring nothing is lost. We establish monitoring and alerting, providing visibility into system health.

Post-transition, we provide a period of intensive support, responding to any issues and ensuring smooth handoff. We then move to your chosen ongoing support model—managed service, project-based support, or on-demand help desk.

What engagement models do you offer for P6 administration?

Managed Service: We take full responsibility for P6 administration including proactive monitoring, preventive maintenance, user support, and system optimisation. This model is ideal for organisations wanting to completely outsource P6 administration.

Managed service includes defined SLAs (Service Level Agreements) specifying system availability targets, response times for incidents, and performance benchmarks. We provide monthly reports showing system health, changes made, and forward-looking recommendations.

Project-Based Support: We support specific initiatives such as P6 implementations, upgrades, large-scale user training, or complex system changes. This model suits organisations with capable in-house administrators who need specialist support for specific projects.

On-Demand Support: You retain your P6 administration team but access our expertise for specific issues, questions, or projects. We provide help desk support, consulting, and specialist knowledge as needed.

Hybrid Models: Many organisations combine elements—managed service for core administration with project-based support for major initiatives, or on-demand support supplementing in-house administration.

We develop engagement models matching your needs, budget, and internal capabilities. We can also adjust models as your organisation evolves.

How do you ensure security and governance for P6 systems?

Security is fundamental to P6 administration. We implement comprehensive security frameworks addressing multiple dimensions.

Authentication: We implement strong authentication mechanisms including multi-factor authentication, preventing unauthorised access even if passwords are compromised.

Authorization: We establish role-based access controls ensuring users access only data and functions relevant to their roles. We implement segregation of duties preventing conflicts of interest (e.g., the same person shouldn't approve and execute transactions).

Encryption: We encrypt data in transit (between user and server) and at rest (in the database). This prevents interception or exposure of sensitive project information.

Audit and Logging: We establish comprehensive audit trails tracking who accessed what data, when, and what changes were made. Audit logs support regulatory compliance and forensic investigation of security incidents.

Network Security: We configure firewalls, access controls, and network segmentation ensuring only authorised traffic reaches P6 systems.

Patch Management: We stay current with security patches, ensuring known vulnerabilities are addressed promptly.

Regular Assessment: We conduct periodic security reviews, vulnerability scanning, and penetration testing identifying and addressing security gaps.

For organisations with specific compliance requirements (healthcare, finance, regulated industries), we implement additional controls meeting compliance standards.

What best practices do you implement for P6 administration?

Documentation: We maintain comprehensive documentation of P6 configuration, procedures, architecture, and decision rationales. This ensures knowledge isn't lost if people change and supports continuous improvement.

Change Management: We implement formal change management procedures for system changes, testing changes thoroughly before production implementation, documenting changes, and communicating impacts.

Capacity Planning: We monitor system capacity (disk space, database growth, user load) and plan for growth proactively rather than reacting when systems run out of capacity.

Performance Optimisation: We regularly review system performance, tuning queries and databases to keep systems responsive as data volumes grow.

Disaster Recovery: We regularly test recovery procedures ensuring you can actually recover from failures, not just in theory. We maintain off-site backups and tested recovery procedures.

Continuous Improvement: We regularly assess P6 configurations and procedures, identifying opportunities to improve security, performance, reliability, or user experience.

Training and Knowledge Transfer: We ensure your team has the knowledge and skills to support P6 systems. We provide training on new features and capabilities, empowering your team to be effective P6 users and administrators.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we expect response to critical P6 issues?

Response time depends on engagement model. Managed service typically provides 1-hour response for critical incidents affecting system availability, 4-hour response for high-priority issues, and 24-hour response for normal requests. SLAs are customised based on your needs.

What happens if we want to switch from our current P6 administrator?

We handle transitions smoothly, assessing existing systems, documenting current configuration and procedures, and providing knowledge transfer from existing administrators. We ensure no systems are lost or left unmonitored during transition.

Can you help with P6 training and user enablement?

Yes. Beyond technical administration, we provide user training on P6 functionality, best practices for schedule management, effective use of reports and dashboards, and adoption support. Training can be general or role-specific (project managers, schedulers, executives).

Do you support P6 integrations with other systems?

We support various integrations including with financial systems (cost data integration), resource management systems, business intelligence platforms, and document management. We can help design and implement integrations improving data flow and reducing manual work.

How do you handle P6 customisation and configuration?

We support P6 customisation within Oracle's supported scope. We can configure custom fields, calculation formulas, reporting structures, and workflows. We advise where customisation is appropriate versus where standard functionality should be adopted.

What's the typical cost for P6 administration services?

Costs vary based on system complexity, user count, deployment type, and engagement model. Managed service typically starts from £1,000 monthly depending on complexity. Project-based support is priced per engagement. On-demand support is available on hourly or retainer basis. We provide detailed proposals based on your specific requirements.
