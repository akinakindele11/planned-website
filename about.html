About Planned Limited

Specialist expertise in project planning, controls, Primavera P6 and Oracle Primavera Cloud administration

Who Is Planned Limited?

Planned Limited is a specialist consulting firm dedicated to delivering exceptional project planning and controls services. With deep expertise in Primavera P6 and Oracle Primavera Cloud, and a track record spanning multiple industries, we help organisations optimise their project delivery and enhance their capabilities.

Our team combines technical mastery with commercial awareness, ensuring that every engagement delivers measurable value to our clients.

Our Mission

To empower organisations with best-in-class project planning, controls, and risk management capabilities, enabling them to deliver projects on time, within budget, and to the desired quality standards.
