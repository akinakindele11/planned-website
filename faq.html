Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

General

What services does Planned Limited offer?

+

We specialise in project planning, project controls, risk analysis, resource management, Primavera P6 administration, and Oracle Primavera Cloud. Our services are tailored to your specific needs and can be delivered as embedded consultancy, project-based work, advisory services, or training.

What industries do you work in?

+

We have proven experience across construction, engineering, infrastructure, and corporate projects. Our cross-industry expertise allows us to bring best practices and insights from multiple sectors to every engagement.

How long have you been in business?

+

Planned Limited is an established firm with extensive experience in project planning and controls. Our team combines decades of collective expertise across multiple industries and project types.

Can you work with small to medium-sized organisations?

+

Absolutely. We work with organisations of all sizes. Our flexible engagement models and cost-effective approach make our services accessible to small and medium-sized companies as well as larger enterprises.

Services & Process

What is your typical project duration?

+

Project durations vary depending on scope and complexity. We undertake short-term advisory engagements, medium-term implementations, and longer-term embedded consultancy roles. We'll discuss your timeline during initial consultation.

How do you define project success?

+

We define success by your project outcomes: delivering on time, within budget, to the desired quality standards. We also measure success by the lasting value created—improved processes, enhanced capabilities, and better decision-making.

Do you help with P6 and Primavera Cloud implementation?

+

Yes, we support Primavera P6 and Oracle Primavera Cloud implementations from planning through to go-live. Our team handles system configuration, data migration, integration, and post-implementation support.

What kind of training do you provide?

+

We deliver customised training on project planning principles, controls methodology, Primavera P6, and Oracle Primavera Cloud. Training can be tailored to different skill levels and delivered either in-person or remotely.

Tools & Technical

What versions of Primavera P6 and Oracle Primavera Cloud do you support?

+

We support all versions of Primavera P6 and Oracle Primavera Cloud, including the latest releases. Our team stays current with the latest developments and can advise on upgrades, cloud migration, and system improvements.

Do you provide on-site support?

+

Yes, we can provide on-site support for embedded consultancy roles, training delivery, and implementation projects. We also offer remote support and advisory services for maximum flexibility.

How do you ensure quality in your deliverables?

+

Quality is embedded in our approach. We follow established methodologies, conduct peer reviews, maintain clear communication with clients, and measure progress against defined success criteria.

Costs & Commercial

How much does Planned Limited's services cost?

+

Our pricing is competitive and tailored to your specific requirements. We offer various engagement models—from day rates for consultancy to fixed-price project fees. Contact us for a detailed proposal based on your needs.

Do you offer payment plans or flexible billing?

+

Yes, we can work with most organisations on flexible billing arrangements. Whether you prefer monthly invoicing, milestone-based payments, or other structures, we're happy to discuss options that suit your cash flow.

What is your typical response time?

+

We typically respond to all enquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters during active engagements, we provide faster response times as agreed in our service terms.

How do I get started with Planned Limited?

+

Simply contact us using our contact form or give us a call. We'll discuss your requirements, understand your challenges, and provide a tailored proposal. There's no obligation—we're here to explore how we can help.
